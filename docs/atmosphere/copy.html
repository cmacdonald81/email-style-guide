Best Pracitices

  • Write preheader text. Preheaders are email descriptions that some clients show near subject lines in email previews. Clients pull this description from the first chunk of text found in the email. To customize the preheader, add a hidden element at the top of the body content. Include an incentive for the user to open the email. There's a range of character limits in preheaders, but it's advisable to keep it within 40-50 characters.
