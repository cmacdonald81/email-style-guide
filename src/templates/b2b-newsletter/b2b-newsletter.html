Custom Tracks & Departments: Working Together in Harmony

Do you have multiple teams in your account? If so, we'd highly recommend taking advantage of our Departments Feature, which has several benefits that will save you time and make your account more organized:

  • Account Management: Appoint one or multiple (there's no limit!) Department Admins to track progress and oversee their specific teams, taking the onus off of you to manage each person
  • Customize Tracks: Allow for those Department Admins to request and assign Custom Tracks that meet the needs of their members
  • Create Groups: Segment your members to easily track the most active teams in the Department Leaderboard
